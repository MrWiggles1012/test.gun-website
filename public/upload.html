Upload a screenshot

This page is used to upload forced screenshots (.tga) from the Medal of Honor: Spearhead server. These screenshots are reviewed by admins to check for cheats.

  1. After a screenshot has been forced, it is saved locally on your PC in your MOHAA game folder.
  2. Find the correct screenshots folder:
    • If you are playing Spearhead: look in your mainta\screenshots folder.
    • If you are playing Breakthrough: look in your maintt\screenshots folder.
  3. Select the appropriate .tga file that the server automatically created and click "Upload screenshot".
Only .tga files are allowed.
