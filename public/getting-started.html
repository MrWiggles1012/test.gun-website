How to Play Spearhead or Breakthrough Online

If you want to play MOH: Spearhead or MOH: Breakthrough online without CD keys, patches, or complicated setup, the steps described below can be followed.


Step 1: Download MOHAA Revival Edition

The Revival Edition includes:

  • Medal of Honor: Allied Assault, Spearhead , and Breakthrough expansions
  • X-null community-made online patch (GameSpy replacement)
  • Widescreen & modern PC support
  • No CD or key required

Download it here


Step 2: Launch Spearhead or Breakthrough

After download and extraction:

  1. If you want to play Spearhead, run the moh_spearhead icon from the folder or desktop shortcut.

  2. If you want to play Breakthrough, run the moh_breakthrough icon from the folder or desktop shortcut.
