Welcome to the G|u|N Clan – MoHAA Spearhead Rifle Only Community

Founded in 2005, the G|u|N Clan has grown from a small group of passionate players into the last standing Rifle Only community in Medal of Honor: Allied Assault Spearhead. Over the years, we’ve built friendships, rivalries, and a unique gaming culture based on skill, respect, and love for this legendary game. This classic game has stood the test of time, and no matter how long we may step away, we always find ourselves coming back to the battlefield. This website was created for all the MoHAA addicts out there — a home for everyone returning to the game they could never truly leave behind. The passion for Spearhead lives on — and we’re here to keep that legacy alive.

This website isn’t just for our clan. It’s for the entire MoHAA Spearhead community — veterans, returning players, and newcomers alike. With no other active forums or centralized hubs left, our commitment remains strong. We’ve created a place where players can connect, share, and keep the spirit of Spearhead alive. Whether you’re part of another clan, a returning veteran, or a complete newbie — you’re welcome here.

This is more than just a clan site, this is the last living hub for MoHAA Spearhead. We’re proud to carry the torch and keep the game alive — but we can’t do it alone. Join us, contribute, play, and be part of a community that still believes in the magic of this classic game.

Everyone is welcome. This is your home too.
