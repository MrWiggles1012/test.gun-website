Join Us

We are a small but passionate Medal of Honor: Allied Assault Spearhead community. You don’t need anything special to join us — good people are always welcome. All we ask is simple:

  • Have passion for MOHAA
  • Be respectful to everyone
  • And most importantly… have fun!

Our community is open to anyone who shares the same love for the game as we do.

Community & Communication

  • We have a public Telegram group for all players on our public server and friends of the clan, where you’ll find info about when people are playing, fun conversations about the game, and a place to share memories of the good old days.

  • We also have a private Telegram group for clan members only. You will have access to it once you become a member.

Keeping MOHAA Alive

We truly believe that Medal of Honor should never die. That’s why we do everything we can to keep the game alive — hosting, playing, and building a welcoming space for fans around the world.

How to Join

If you share the same passion and want to be part of our clan, just contact one of our leaders on Telegram. They’ll guide you through the simple joining process and welcome you into the family.
